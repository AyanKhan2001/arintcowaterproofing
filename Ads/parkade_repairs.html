What we do

Roaron Construction completes structural repairs and protection to parkades / parking garages.

This includes:
- floor crack repairs / sealing
- stall repairs (floor and overhead / soffit)
- rebar rehabilitation
- coatings
- structural enhancements
- fiberwrap

Why worry?

A suspended slab structure is generally an expensive one, especially to repair.  It is always best to address damaged areas or areas that promote damage quickly.  

A simple crack fill or membrane repair is relatively inexpensive compared to concrete replacement and rebar work. Better to spend $20,000 now than wait and have to spend $100,000 5 yrs later.

Why Use Roaron?

Roaron Construction specializes in patchwork, concrete repairs, polymer modified concrete and coating prep work.

We work closely with engineers to ensure not only are you getting the right repairs, but the best value repairs.

SUSPENDED SLAB CARE

PROTECT - RENEW - INVEST

Concrete is a very tough product, unfortunatly steel doesn't share its resistance to corrosion.  Steel in a suspended slab is doing all the work.  It is difficult to walk and drive on a web of steel, so concrete provides an excellent surface for this.  The downside is concrete cracks easily and unprotected steel is highly susceptable to corrosion.  Once corrosion takes place, steel loses its strength and the expansion of the steel from rusting will break the concrete around it.

Neglect of a structure can lead to serious life threatening injuries.
