Professional sealcoating services using the latest quality sealants on the market.

What is it?

Sealcoating is the application of an asphalt product to coat the surface of an existing pavement to bond and support the surface structure while maintaining a barrier to harmful elements.

Sealcoating Costs?

Sealcoating costs will vary on size, details for edging, cleaning, prep, surface profile and product type.

As a general rule, a sealcoat should start at approximately $0.40 per sq.ft. for large jobs and go up from there.

Why use Roaron?

Roaron Construction has installed sealcoats on the market for residential, commercial and industrial clients since 1989. We have tested most products on the market and have years of monitoring lifespan, wear, and other performance results of sealers.

We know our stuff and how to apply for our climate.
